Hair consultation

Discover what truly suits you

Not sure yet what style you’re going for? During a hair diagnosis, we take a close look at your hair texture, scalp, and natural features. Together, we’ll discover which style, length, or color complements you.

I take the time to listen to what matters to you. I’ll give honest advice on what your hair needs, what’s achievable, and how to maintain it at home.

A hair diagnosis, is when you’re ready for a change or simply want to understand your hair better.

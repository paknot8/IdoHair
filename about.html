Hi, I’m Ido

Story about Ido

From a young age, I was fascinated by hair, always styling, braiding, and playing with it long before I knew it would become my life’s passion. What began as childhood curiosity grew into a career that has taken me around the world and shaped who I am today.

After years of working and learning internationally, from London and Italy to the U.S. and Israel, I found my home in Amsterdam. At 27, I opened my own studio in the heart of De Pijp, where I still welcome clients from all over the world. My approach is personal and intuitive; I see every client as a unique canvas and every haircut as a collaboration.

For me, hair is more than beauty, it’s identity, confidence, and self-expression. Whether it’s a bold transformation, a natural refresh, or a creative color mix, my goal is always the same: to help people feel like the best version of themselves.
